Palestine

● Ongoing Conflict

For decades, Palestinians have lived under occupation, displacement, and siege. Since October 2023, the war in Gaza has become one of the deadliest humanitarian crises in modern history — over 30,000 lives lost, hospitals bombed, entire neighbourhoods reduced to rubble. Families torn apart. Children who never knew a day without war.

The Palestinian people have been fighting for their right to exist, to return to their land, to live in dignity. Their flag, their olive trees, their keffiyeh — these are not just symbols. They are survival.

How you can help: Amplify their voices. Boycott complicit brands. Support humanitarian organisations. And wear the flag — because visibility matters.

Uyghurs — East Turkestan

● Ongoing Oppression

More than one million Uyghur Muslims have been detained in what China calls "re-education camps" in the Xinjiang region. Families are separated without warning. Mosques are demolished. Speaking your language or praying can get you imprisoned. Surveillance is total — facial recognition, DNA collection, forced sterilisation.

The world's largest internment of an ethnic group since World War II is happening right now — yet most people have never heard of it. The Uyghur people are losing their culture, their faith, their freedom, and their children. Silence is complicity.

How you can help: Speak about it. Pressure governments to act. Support Uyghur diaspora organisations. Share the truth.

DR Congo

● Forgotten Crisis

The Democratic Republic of Congo has been called the "world's most neglected crisis." For over 25 years, armed militias and foreign-backed rebel groups have terrorised civilians in the east of the country. Millions have been displaced. Women and children are the primary targets of systematic sexual violence used as a weapon of war.

The DRC holds some of the world's richest mineral reserves — coltan, cobalt, gold. The very minerals in our phones and laptops. The world profits from Congo's resources while its people bleed. Over 6 million displaced. More than 500,000 lives lost. Almost no international coverage.

How you can help: Say their names. Question where your minerals come from. Support Congolese-led organisations fighting for justice and protection.
Spread Awareness
🇸🇩

Sudan

● Active Civil War

Since April 2023, Sudan has been torn apart by a brutal civil war between the Sudanese Armed Forces and the paramilitary Rapid Support Forces. In a matter of months, it became the world's largest displacement crisis — over 10 million people forced from their homes, with no end in sight.

Khartoum, once a thriving capital, is now a battlefield. Entire cities have been ethnically cleansed. Famine is spreading. Hospitals are targeted. Women are raped in the streets. The international community has largely looked away from what experts warn could become genocide. Sudan is screaming — and the world is mostly silent.

How you can help: Share their story. Pressure world leaders to act. Donate to Sudan emergency relief. Wear the flag and keep the conversation alive.
