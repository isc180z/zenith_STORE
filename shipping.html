Shipping & Returns

Free shipping on every order. A 30-day no-questions-asked return policy.

Shipping Options

We offer several delivery options to fit your schedule. All domestic orders include free standard shipping.

Method Delivery Time Cost
Standard Shipping 3–7 Business Days FREE
Express Shipping 1–2 Business Days $9.99
International Standard 7–14 Business Days $14.99
International Express 3–5 Business Days $29.99

Order Tracking

Once your order ships, you'll receive a shipping confirmation email with a tracking number. Tracking is usually active within 24 hours of dispatch. You can track your parcel directly on our carrier's website at any time.

If you haven't received tracking information within 3 business days of your order, please contact us at support@zenith.store.

Processing Time

All orders are processed within 1–2 business days (Monday–Friday, excluding public holidays). Orders placed after 2pm EST will typically be processed the next business day.

International Shipping

We ship to over 40 countries worldwide. Please note:

  • Import duties, taxes, and customs fees are the recipient's responsibility
  • International delivery times may vary depending on customs clearance
  • Some regions may not be eligible for express service
  • Tracking may have fewer updates once the parcel leaves our country

Returns Policy

Not happy? No problem. We offer a 30-day hassle-free return on all items. Items must be:

  • Returned within 30 days of the delivery date
  • In original, unused condition with all accessories included
  • In original packaging (box, inserts, manuals)

Items that are damaged through misuse, or custom/personalized products, are not eligible for return.

How to Return an Item

1

Initiate Your Return

Email returns@zenith.store with your order number and reason for return. We'll respond with a prepaid return label within 24 hours.

2

Pack Your Item

Securely pack your item in its original packaging. Attach the prepaid return label to the outside of the box. Drop off at any authorized carrier location.

3

We Receive & Inspect

Once we receive your return, our team will inspect the item within 1–2 business days. You'll receive an email once the inspection is complete.

4

Refund Issued

Your refund will be processed to the original payment method within 2–3 business days after inspection. Depending on your bank, funds typically appear within 3–5 additional days.

Exchanges

We don't offer direct exchanges at this time. If you'd like a different product, please return your original item for a refund and place a new order.

Damaged or Defective Items

If your item arrives damaged or is defective, contact us at support@zenith.store within 48 hours of delivery. Please include:

  • Your order number
  • A description of the issue
  • Clear photos of the damage or defect

We'll arrange a replacement or full refund — no return required for damaged goods.

Need help with a return?
Email returns@zenith.store or reach our support team at support@zenith.store. We're here Mon–Fri, 9am–6pm EST.
