Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Zenith products, orders, and policies.

Orders & Payment

Simply browse our products, click Add to Cart, then open your cart and click Checkout. Fill in your shipping and payment details and submit your order. You'll receive a confirmation email within minutes.

We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Discover). All transactions are encrypted with 256-bit SSL. We do not store your card details.

Orders can be modified or cancelled within 2 hours of placement — after that, they enter fulfilment and cannot be changed. Please contact support@zenith.store immediately if you need to make changes.

A confirmation email is sent to the address you provided at checkout. If you don't see it within 10 minutes, check your spam or junk folder. Still nothing? Contact us and we'll resend it.

Shipping & Delivery

Standard shipping takes 3–7 business days. Express shipping (1–2 business days) is available at checkout. International orders typically arrive in 7–14 business days depending on customs clearance.

Yes! All domestic orders ship for free with standard delivery. Express and international shipping are charged at cost and shown transparently at checkout.

Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number by email. You can use that number on our carrier's website to see real-time delivery updates. Tracking is usually active within 24 hours of dispatch.

Yes, we ship to over 40 countries worldwide. International customers may be subject to import duties and taxes depending on their country's regulations — these are the buyer's responsibility.

Returns & Refunds

We offer a 30-day hassle-free return policy. Items must be in their original, unused condition with all original packaging. Simply initiate a return from your order confirmation email.

Once we receive and inspect your return, refunds are processed within 2–3 business days. Depending on your bank, funds may take an additional 3–5 days to appear on your statement.

We're sorry to hear that! Please take photos of the damage and email us at support@zenith.store within 48 hours of delivery. We'll send a replacement or issue a full refund immediately, no return required.

Products & Warranty

Yes. All Zenith products include a 1-year manufacturer's warranty covering defects in materials and workmanship. Extended 2-year warranties can be purchased at checkout.

Compatibility varies by product. Each product page lists supported platforms and devices. Our audio accessories are universally compatible with iOS, Android, and PC. The charging pad supports all Qi-certified devices.

You can reach our team at support@zenith.store or via live chat on our homepage (Mon–Fri, 9am–6pm EST). We typically respond within a few hours.

Our support team is happy to help. Email us at support@zenith.store and we'll get back to you within 24 hours.
